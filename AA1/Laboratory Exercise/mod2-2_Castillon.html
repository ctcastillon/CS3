Spirited Away

It is a tale about a young girl named Chihiro, who visited an unseeingly abandoned amusement park with her parents. After her parents turned into pigs, Chihiro meets the mysterious Haku.Who explains that the park is a resort for supernatural beings who needs a break from their time spent on the earthly realm, and that she must work there to free herself and her parents.
