Han Sooyoung

  • Han Sooyoung exudes a lot of self-assurance in both her appearance and her abilities.
  • She is a very talented writer who calls herself a genius, and she has the money to prove it: before "Ways of Survival" became a reality, she was a very well-known author who had amassed enormous wealth from her works.
  • Her self-assurance does, however, sometimes get in the way as she exhibits stubbornness and takes a little longer to accept her mistakes.
  • This character's portrayal of a flawed human being struck me as realistic; while she may come out as flawless, self-assured, and helpful, everyone has a secret side that they would rather keep hidden.
  • Just remember that human beings are capable of growth and that relationships with others need time to develop.
  • Even though she makes every effort to protect the people she cares about, she will criticize them.
  • She regularly demonstrates her extreme independence and idealistic outlook on the future.
  • She puts in a lot of effort to get the result she wants.
