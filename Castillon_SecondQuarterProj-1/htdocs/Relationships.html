RELATIONSHIPS

Yoo Joonghyuk

Yoo Joonghyuk served as Kim Dokja's inspiration while he was growing up. with kim Dokja repeatedly uttering the phrase'I am Yoo Joonghyuk', he strives to be courageous in order to face reality. A fictional figure named Yoo Joonghyuk served as Kim Dokja's father, brother, and closest friend as he grew up without any parents to turn to because his mother was imprisoned for killing his abusive father. Kim Dokja did this in order to cope. They evolved into Life and Death Companions in the post-apocalyptic world, collaborating to address numerous challenges. Despite their love-hate relationship, Kim Dokja regards Yoo Joonghyuk as the coolest person he knows and passionately admires him.

Appearance

Yoo Joonghyuk has wide black eyes with thin double eyelids and thick eyebrows. When the talent [Sage's Eye] is used, his right eye begins to glow golden. His short, black hair is somewhat waved. He has a strong jawline, a prominent nose ridge, and tanned skin. He is really muscular; his thighs and biceps are especially well-toned.

Han Sooyoung

Han Sooyoung initially had a hostile relationship with Kim Dokja, but when Han Sooyoung changed the narrative and led the prophets, their friendship grew closer. In the beginning, Kim Dokja continually makes fun of Han Sooyoung for allegedly copying passages from his favorite book, despite her repeated denials. Han Sooyoung eventually joins "Kim Dokja's Company." As they fight together, they start to actually appreciate each other's skills and shortcomings despite their frequent banter and insults.

Appearance

Han Sooyoung is able to alter the look of her Avatar clones, changing her appearance as she goes. She is regarded as being fairly attractive in her real form, which is petite, slim, and well-proportioned. Her skin is white, and she has a small face with pointed teeth. Her eyes are big, with slanted upward corners, and one of them has a beauty mark. She has black, short, fine hair that only reaches her shoulders.
