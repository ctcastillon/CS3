Dokja was a typical office worker who simply read his favorite web novel, "Three Ways to Survive the Apocalypse," for pleasure. However, when the novel unexpectedly became a reality, he is the only person who is aware of how the world would end. Armed with this knowledge, Dokja changes the direction of the narrative and his perception of the world.
